About

Calvin JR. was conceived in October 2014. Our mission at CJR is to create timepieces that are bold and original. “An Unexpected Journey” to our team, our supporters, and ultimately to be a unique brand in the watch industry.

CJR strive to employ unconventional design concepts and utilize cutting-edge materials. From the initial concept to our final product we aim for uncompromising quality and affordable timepieces.

CJR VELOCITA Series

VELOCITA - The Automatic Series

The Automatic Series also include a skeleton sidewall that is precisely machined and polished with the utmost attention to detail and clean, polished finish. CJR Automatic Series are available in two models: Carbon utilizes the lightweight & strength of carbon fiber in a subtle black/red color; in contrast, C3P0-1 unleashes the glamorous design and features of CJR on your wrist.

VELOCITA - Series 1

Series 1 timepieces include the precision machined sidewall with a fine polished and platted skeleton side structure. Series 1 are available in Spacecraft dark grey/neon and USO Aquatic blue.

VELOCITA - Series 2

The Series 2 timepiece includes a solid skeleton sidewall with sandblasted finish. It is available in Army. The Fire Red model’s solid sidewall will be filled with red enamel.
